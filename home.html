Sodra Teatern

Södra Teatern is one of Sweden's oldest active theaters. The current theater was designed by architect Johan Fredrik Åbom (1817-1900) and was inaugurated in 1859. The building also consists of five bars and the Mosebacke Etablissement restaurant. Stora Scen is the main stage. Mosebacketerrassen is an outdoor terrace.

Slakthuset

For several years now, the club venue "Slakthuset" has been hosten in an old slaughterhouse just south of central Stockholm. Here you will fill find Stockholms finest dancing and clubbing to internationally established SuperDJs, and up-and-coming local acts. The venue is located in the meatpacking close to the subway station Globen and Gullmarsplan.

Fasching

Fasching is a concert venue, club, restaurant and bar centrally located on Kungsgatan 63 in Stockholm. Since its inception in 1977, Fasching has grown to become Scandinavia's largest organizer of jazz and the club is well known both in the country and internationally.
