Best clubs in Stockholm

A local guide to Stockholm's best r&b and hip hop clubs

Hilma

The kind of place where the food and drink tickle your senses and the Art follows you home. The kind of night where you can free your mind on the dance floor and let your senses guide you. Take a swing in Hilma’s artfully seductive surroundings, dance the night away with a drink in your hand or get inked for ever and always - the choice is yours.

Spy Bar

Sweden’s leading entertainment group, Stureplansgruppen operates bars and nightclubs in Stockholm, Visby and Gothenburg. We are totally committed to deliver first-class entertainment experiences. Visit a nightclub or bar and you’ll enjoy a unique experience with regard to atmosphere, music, food, beverage and ambience. All in a safe and secure environment.

Sodra Teatern

Södra Teatern is one of Sweden's oldest active theaters. The current theater was designed by architect Johan Fredrik Åbom (1817-1900) and was inaugurated in 1859. It has two restaurants, outdoor terraces and seven stages. The building also consists of five bars and the Mosebacke Etablissement restaurant. Stora Scen is the main stage. Mosebacketerrassen is an outdoor terrace.

Slakthuset

For several years now, the club venue "Slakthuset" has been hosten in an old slaughterhouse just south of central Stockholm. Here you will fill find Stockholms finest dancing and clubbing to internationally established SuperDJs, and up-and-coming local acts. The venue is located in the meatpacking close to the subway station Globen and Gullmarsplan.
